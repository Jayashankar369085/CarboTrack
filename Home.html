Did you know?
Renewable energy sources like wind and solar power are growing at an unprecedented rate, with nearly 72% of all new power capacity additions globally in 2020 coming from renewables. This growth is driven by technological advancements, decreasing costs.

Did you know?
The renewable energy sector is a major driver of employment, providing jobs for more than 11 million people worldwide as of 2018. These jobs span a wide range of activities, including manufacturing, installation, maintenance, and research and development.

Did you know?
Transitioning to renewable energy could reduce global carbon emissions by up to 70% by 2050. This significant reduction is essential for limiting global temperature rise to 1.5Â°C above pre-industrial levels, as outlined in the Paris Agreement.

Did you know?
The average global carbon footprint per person is approximately 4.8 metric tons of CO2 annually, but this number varies significantly between countries.
