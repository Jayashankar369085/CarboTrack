15 August 2024
National Tree Planting Day
National Tree Planting Day is a day dedicated to the planting of trees and raising awareness about the importance of trees in our environment. It is celebrated in many countries around the world to promote reforestation and combat deforestation.

22 September 2024
World Car-Free Day
World Car-Free Day is an annual event celebrated on September 22nd in cities around the world. It encourages people to use alternative modes of transportation such as walking, cycling, or public transit to reduce air pollution and traffic congestion.

10 October 2024
International Day of Climate Action
The International Day of Climate Action is a global day of awareness and action on climate change. It aims to mobilize individuals, communities, and governments to take urgent and meaningful action to address the climate crisis.

5 November 2024
National Recycling Day
National Recycling Day is observed on November 15th to raise awareness about the importance of recycling and encourage people to recycle their waste materials. It promotes environmental sustainability and helps reduce waste in landfills.
